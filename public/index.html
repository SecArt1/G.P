Secure, Reliable, Yours

BioTrack leverages the power and security of Google Firebase to store your health data. When you record a measurement, it's saved directly to your private cloud space, accessible only through your authenticated account.

  • Real-time Sync: Your data is available across devices (if applicable).
  • Robust Security: Built on Google's infrastructure with strong authentication rules.
  • User Controlled: You own your data. View, manage, and delete your records anytime.
