BioTrack Features

Explore the tools BioTrack provides to help you monitor and understand your health data effectively.

Effortless Data Entry

Quickly log your vital signs with a clean and simple interface. Record:

  • Heart Rate: Beats per minute (BPM).
  • Blood Pressure: Both Systolic and Diastolic values.
  • Weight: In your preferred unit (kg/lb - *if unit selection is implemented*).
  • Blood Sugar: Glucose levels (mg/dL or mmol/L - *if unit selection is implemented*).
  • Oxygen Saturation: SpO2 percentage.
  • Notes: Add context to your readings (e.g., "after workout", "fasting").

Insightful Visualizations

Don't just see numbers, see trends. Our interactive charts help you visualize your health data over time.

  • Line Charts: Track individual metrics like heart rate or weight over selected periods.
  • Time Filters: Easily switch between viewing data for the last week, month, year, or all time.
  • Tap for Details: Tap on chart points to see the exact reading and date.

Comprehensive History & Summary

Access your complete health record and get quick statistical insights.

  • List View: See all your recorded measurements chronologically, filterable by type and time.
  • Detailed View: Tap any entry to see all associated details and notes.
  • Summary Statistics: Understand your data better with calculated Average, Median, Minimum, and Maximum values for the selected period and metric.
  • Data Management: Easily delete incorrect entries.

Personalized Settings

Customize the app to fit your preferences.

  • Theme Selection: Switch between a comfortable Light Mode and a battery-saving Dark Mode.
  • Language Options: Use the app in English or Arabic (العربية).
  • Profile Management: Keep your basic profile information up-to-date.

Secure and Private

Your health data is sensitive, and we treat it that way.

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure login ensures only you can access your account.
  • Firestore Security Rules: Data is stored in the cloud with rules preventing unauthorized access.
  • User Control: You have full control over your data within the app.
