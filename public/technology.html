How BioTrack Works: Secure & Simple

We believe technology should simplify, not complicate. Here's a look at how BioTrack securely handles your health data.

Your Data in the Cloud

When you enter a health reading (like heart rate or weight) into the BioTrack app:

  1. The app securely connects to Google Firebase using your authenticated account.
  2. Your data point (value, type, timestamp, notes) is encrypted during transmission.
  3. It's stored in your dedicated space within Firebase Firestore, a secure NoSQL cloud database.

Security & Privacy Focused

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication (Email/Password) to ensure only you can log in to your account. Password reset emails are handled securely.
  • Authorization: Firestore Security Rules are configured so that you can only read and write your *own* health data. No other user can access your records.
  • Infrastructure: We rely on Google Cloud's robust and secure infrastructure, benefiting from their expertise in data protection.
  • No Third-Party Sharing (Unless Required): We don't sell your personal health data. We only share information as outlined in our Privacy Policy, primarily for app functionality (like cloud storage) or if legally required.

Built with Flutter

BioTrack is developed using Flutter, Google's UI toolkit for building beautiful, natively compiled applications for mobile from a single codebase. This allows for a consistent experience and faster development of new features.
