Frequently Asked Questions

Is BioTrack free to use?

Yes, the core features of BioTrack for tracking and viewing your health data are currently free to use.

What health metrics can I track?

You can currently track Heart Rate, Blood Pressure (Systolic & Diastolic), Weight, Blood Sugar, and Oxygen Saturation (SpO2).

Where is my data stored? Is it secure?

Your data is securely stored in the cloud using Google Firebase Firestore. We use Firebase Authentication and Security Rules to ensure only you can access your data. Please see our How It Works page and Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I export my data?

Currently, data export is not a built-in feature, but it's something we are considering for future updates. Your data is accessible within the app's history and summary views.

Does BioTrack provide medical advice?

No. BioTrack is an informational tool for tracking your health data. It does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for any health concerns or before making any decisions related to your health.

How do I change the language or theme?

You can change the language (English/Arabic) and theme (Light/Dark) in the Settings section within the BioTrack app.

How do I delete my account or data?

You can delete individual readings from the history list within the app. To request full account and data deletion, please contact us through the support channel (details to be added, e.g., support email).

What platforms is BioTrack available on?

BioTrack is currently available for Android. An iOS version may be considered in the future. (Update links on homepage when available).
